# Storytelling Report Examples

This folder contains outlines and starter structures for **Fusedash** storytelling reports.

Storytelling reports turn dashboard views into a clear narrative: what changed, what caused it, and what to do next.

## Related product page
- Storytelling: https://fusedash.ai/storytelling

## What good data storytelling includes
- The decision context (what the report is for)
- The headline change (up/down, magnitude, timeframe)
- The drivers (segments, cohorts, regions, products)
- The impact (revenue, cost, risk, SLA, pipeline)
- The next step (actions and owners)

## Recommended report formats
- Weekly KPI update (wins, risks, next actions)
- Month-end performance review (variance and drivers)
- Campaign recap (what worked, what did not, what to test next)
- Forecast review (assumptions vs actuals, reforecast triggers)

If you add a report outline, keep it specific and decision-focused, not just descriptive.
