# Storytelling Report Examples

Storytelling examples for turning dashboards into a narrative that stakeholders can act on.

Related page: https://fusedash.ai/storytelling  
Also useful: AI Chat https://fusedash.ai/chat

## 1) Weekly KPI update (leadership)
**Structure:** headline change → drivers → risks → next actions  
**Include:** top 3 wins, top 3 risks, what changed vs last week  
Best for: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/business-leaders

## 2) Month-end performance review (finance)
**Structure:** forecast vs actual → variance drivers → corrective actions  
**Include:** run-rate, budget remaining, variance by category/cost center  
Best for: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/finance-teams

## 3) Client reporting summary (agency)
**Structure:** goals → results → what changed → next tests  
**Include:** spend, ROAS/CAC, lead quality, best and worst campaigns  
Best for: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/agencies-and-client-reporting

## 4) Campaign recap (marketing)
**Structure:** objective → performance by segment → learnings → next moves  
**Include:** channel mix, conversion rate shifts, landing page insights  
Best for: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/marketing-teams

## 5) Ops incident recap (operations)
**Structure:** what happened → impact → root causes → prevention  
**Include:** incident volume, SLA breaches, time-to-resolution, backlog trend  
Best for: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/operations-and-logistics

Tip: A good story answers one question: what decision should we make next.
