# Dataset Examples

Small dataset ideas you can publish in this repository to support dashboard, map, and storytelling examples.

Related pages:
- Dashboards: https://fusedash.ai/dashboards
- Charts: https://fusedash.ai/charts
- Maps: https://fusedash.ai/maps

## 1) Ecommerce KPI sample (daily)
**Columns:** date, sessions, add_to_cart, checkout_start, orders, revenue, aov, refunds, country, device, channel

## 2) SaaS funnel and retention sample (weekly)
**Columns:** week, signups, activated, retained_users, churned_users, mrr_new, mrr_expansion, mrr_churn, plan, cohort

## 3) Finance variance sample (monthly)
**Columns:** month, forecast, actual, variance, variance_pct, department, cost_center, category

## 4) Operations SLA sample (daily)
**Columns:** date, orders, delivered, late_deliveries, avg_delivery_time, sla_breaches, warehouse, carrier, region

## 5) Region performance sample (for maps)
**Columns:** date, country, region, city, revenue, orders, customers, lat, lon

Rule: Do not include personal data or sensitive client data. Keep datasets small and easy to understand.
