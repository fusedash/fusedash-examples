# Real-Time Monitoring Examples

This folder contains real-time monitoring patterns for **Fusedash**. Real-time dashboards work best as operational tools: they surface issues early and keep teams aligned during fast-moving periods.

## Related product page
- Real-time interface: https://fusedash.ai/real-time-interface

## What you will find here
- Real-time dashboard patterns by team
- KPI guardrails and leading indicators to monitor live
- Data quality checks that prevent false alarms

## What to track live (rule of thumb)
Track signals that change decisions in the moment:
- leading indicators (early signals)
- operational guardrails (risk, pacing, SLA)
- data freshness and pipeline health

Avoid tracking lagging metrics live when you cannot act on them immediately.

## How to use
Start with 6 to 12 live KPIs, define thresholds, and include one “top movers” view so the team sees the drivers, not only the alert.
