# Map Examples

Map example ideas for Fusedash. Use maps when geography explains performance or when coverage and capacity matter.

Related page: https://fusedash.ai/maps

## 1) Revenue by region (choropleth)
**Goal:** compare performance across regions at a glance  
**Metric:** revenue (and revenue per customer if needed)  
**Drilldowns:** region → city → account / store  
Use with: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/ecommerce-dashboard

## 2) Operations coverage and gaps (territory map)
**Goal:** see underserved areas and prioritize expansion  
**Metric:** orders, deliveries, response time, capacity  
**Drilldowns:** service area, route, depot / warehouse  
Use with: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/operations-and-logistics

## 3) SLA risk zones (heatmap)
**Goal:** detect clusters of delays, incidents, or backlog  
**Metric:** late deliveries, incident count, cycle time  
**Drilldowns:** time window, route type, carrier  
Use with: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/operations-teams

## 4) Campaign performance by location (point map)
**Goal:** identify high-performing cities and wasted spend areas  
**Metric:** leads, CAC, ROAS, conversion rate  
**Drilldowns:** campaign, audience, device, time  
Use with: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/marketing-teams

## 5) Financial performance by country (choropleth)
**Goal:** track margin, cost, and revenue differences across markets  
**Metric:** gross margin %, CAC, ARPU, refund rate  
**Drilldowns:** market, channel, product line  
Use with: https://fusedash.ai/solutions/finance-teams

Tip: Pair maps with a table or trend line so viewers can validate what the map is showing.
